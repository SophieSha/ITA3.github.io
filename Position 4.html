Cyber Security Consultant

  • 6 month contract
  • Immediate Start
  • Train/metro related
  • Melbourne CBD location

About role

We are looking forward to working in partnership with a great cyber security consultant for building up and maintaining a secure operating system for our new train related project.

Skills & qualifications

  • Tertiary level qualification in relevant area
  • Minimum 3 years of relevant experience
  • Knowledge of a wide range of operating systems and authentication mechanisms
  • Hold recognized security certificates such as CISSP, SSCP, CISA, Security+ or CSX, ISO27001:2013
  • Familiar with current information security practices.
  • Ability to identify security threats, vulnerabilities and risk to public transportation application.
  • Have experience in performing deep dives, analysis and making recommendations
  • Have good written communication skills and excellent willingness of cooperation.

Responsibilities

  • Build up the security system of our brand new mobile application
  • Manage and monitor the security system
  • Fix vulnerabilities and monitoring intrusion attempts
  • Solve security issues promptly during developing process
