About role

A great opportunity is currently open for an experienced UI/UX designer who is able to combine art and computer science together to improve our product users’ experience. We value good UI/UX and we are looking forward to a confident and talented designer to join our growing team.

Skills & qualifications

  • Tertiary level qualification in relevant area
  • Minimum 3 years UI/UX design experience
  • Able to understand detailed product needs
  • Design high quality mobile application which prioritizes UX
  • A good design portfolio would be helpful
  • Familiar with UI/UX design tools such as Invision, ProtoIO, Balsamiq, etc.
  • Team player, good communication skills

Responsibilities

  • Design product with user-centered design principles
  • Make good prototypes and wireframes, test with right user groups
  • Constantly improve the application design to ensure it meets user’s needs
  • Keep the product design consistent during developing process
  • Cooperate with other project members and ensure meet deadline on time
